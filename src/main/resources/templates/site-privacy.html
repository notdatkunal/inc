Cookies.
 
 When you visit the Site or use our services, we utilize "cookies" to monitor certain kinds of information. Cookies are little files that are placed on your computer that let us identify returning and new visitors to the site and to count the number of people that visit it. In order to keep developing and enhancing our service offering, they also let us track use and retain user preferences.

Cookies for sessions and persistence.
 
 Temporary session cookies are deleted when your browser is closed. Persistent cookies stay on your computer until you remove them manually or they expire after a predetermined amount of time. Persistent cookies are mostly used for performance metrics by us or a reliable partner, and to facilitate the login procedure into your account. Cookies from first parties and others may be session-based or permanent.

Cookies from the First Party.
 
 We install first-party cookies on your computer for a number of reasons, such as monitoring the performance of the website, calculating the number of unique visitors and the duration of their stay, and gauging the level of popularity of particular site features. The PHPSESSID Session Cookie, adult_cookie over age consent, Logged_cookie when you do log in, and privacy_cookie agreed cookies on the website are some of the first-party cookies used by Incallup.

Web beacons.
 
 "Web beacons"—also referred to as "clear gifs" and "pixel tags"—are tiny, transparent visual images that are frequently used in tandem with cookies to better customize the Site for our users and gather a restricted amount of visitor data. Web beacons may also be used by us in email correspondence to track consumer behavior. The web beacons are not connected to any data that would allow us to ascertain the true identify of any individual.

Cookies from third parties.
 
 Only third-party statistical cookies are used by us. These third parties only obtain anonymous data through cookies and beacons, which prevents them from knowing who you really are. Among the third-party cookies used by Incallup are Hotjar and Google Analytics.
